Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Sprechstimme website ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles information when you visit sprechstim.me (the "Website").

Information We Collect

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our Website. When you visit our Website, the following information may be collected by Cloudflare:

  • IP Address: Your IP address is sent to Cloudflare for the purpose of analytics processing. Cloudflare anonymizes this data and does not store your full IP address.
  • Page Views: Information about which pages you visit on our Website.
  • Referrer: The website that referred you to our Website, if applicable.
  • Browser and Device Information: General information about your browser type and device.

How We Use This Information

We use the analytics data to:

  • Understand how visitors use our Website
  • Improve our Website's content and user experience
  • Monitor the performance and availability of our Website

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For visitors from the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland, we process this data under the legal basis of legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) of the GDPR). Our legitimate interests include:

  • Understanding website usage to improve our services
  • Ensuring the security and proper functioning of our Website
  • Analyzing trends to better serve our users

We have assessed that these interests do not override your fundamental rights and freedoms, particularly as Cloudflare Web Analytics is a privacy-focused analytics solution that does not use cookies and anonymizes IP addresses.

Data Retention

Cloudflare Web Analytics does not store your IP address. Analytics data is retained by Cloudflare in aggregated, anonymized form. We do not have access to individual-level data that could identify you personally.

Cookies

Cloudflare Web Analytics does not use cookies. Our Website does not set any tracking cookies for analytics purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our Website uses the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare: For web analytics and content delivery. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts: For typography. Google Privacy Policy
  • GitHub: Our source code and documentation are hosted on GitHub. GitHub Privacy Statement
  • jsDelivr CDN: For delivering the Pyodide Python runtime in our playground. jsDelivr Privacy Policy

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to object to processing
  • The right to data portability

However, as we do not store personally identifiable information, we may not be able to identify or retrieve data specific to you.

Children's Privacy

Our Website is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
