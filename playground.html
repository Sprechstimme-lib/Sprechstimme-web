Quick API

sp.new("synth_name") sp.create("synth_name", wavetype=sp.waves.sine) sp.play("synth_name", "C4", duration=1.0)

Wavetypes: sine, sawtooth, square, triangle
Notes: "C4", "A4", or MIDI numbers
